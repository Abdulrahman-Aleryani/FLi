Kids - English Course

Prepare your kids to speak English confidently using our proven method. We enable children to build the confidence and skills needed to speak English fluently through fun and engaging activities.

Start learning →

How does the English course work?

We guarantee your kids will learn English with our proven method. Full-access experience: study in English-only learning centers and join international students in our digital classroom.

What will I learn?

Based on your current level. 20 levels from beginner to advanced, aligned to the CEFR. Fun learning content and videos to develop all language skills with interactive activities powered by AI.

How long is the course?

Personalized plans based on your starting level and goals, e.g. 9, 12 or 18 month courses. Study 3 hours per week. Go from Elementary level (A2) to Intermediate (B1) in 6 months by following our method.
